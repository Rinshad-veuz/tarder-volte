Your trading robots and signal subscriptions can operate 24/7 even when the computer is turned off. The MetaTrader Virtual Hosting service is similar to a Virtual Private Server service, although it is a superior and more suitable service for addressing the needs and challenges that a trader can face when trading on financial markets.

The MetaTrader Virtual Hosting is a simple and user-friendly service, enabling traders to rent a server directly from the MetaTrader platform without any additional settings or adjustments needed. Advantages of the Virtual Hosting (Forex VPS):

  • 24/7 operation of trading robots and signal subscriptions
  • Minimum network latency to broker's trade server
  • Renting a Forex VPS straight from MetaTrader in a few clicks
  • No additional configurations or adjustments
  • 24 hours free trial is provided for you to test the service
